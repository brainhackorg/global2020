    Wacky brain explosions Vol II.

    Last updated on Oct 9, 2020

    Title: Wacky brain explosions Vol II.

    Project lead: @ofgulban - Omer Faruk Gulban

    Timezone: Late Europe to Americas timezone.

    Hub: Europe, Middle East and Africa (but I can only work on this project in the evening/night, therefore I will be more active in the evening/night).

    Description: Are you asking to yourself “how can the year 2020 get more interesting?”. The answer is simple: by using a particle simulator to explode some brains at the comfort of your home, in a completely safe virtual environment.

    After concluding my previous hackaton project with “more research is needed”, here I am planning to continue exploding more brains in many ways. All in the name of neuro-science, of course!

    Link to project: Slowest particle simulator on earth

    Mattermost handle: brainexplosions

    Goals for the OHBM Brainhack This project was initiated in OpenMR Benelux 2020 Nijmegen (please see this link for further details). In this event, I am planning to work on adding multi-phase explosions.

    Good first issues:

    1. Install Slowest particle simulator on earth.
    2. Use it to explode a brain image that you want to explode. Some options might be:
      • A bad subject who moved too much.
      • Your supervisor’s brain.
      • Maybe even your own brain.

    Skills: Python, enjoying visual effects, and optionally C, C++, or Cython for understanding the source material mentioned in here (please see this link for further details).

    Chat channel: Mattermost channel brainexplosions

    Video channel: TODO: Add when prepared.
